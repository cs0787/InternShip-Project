Who We Are

Think Global,
Act Local

NayePankh Foundation is a non-governmental organisation with a burning desire to uplift society and create a better world for all. Rooted in the belief that service to mankind is service to God, we work across UP and beyond to make real, lasting change.

Founded and driven by students, we prove that age is no barrier to impact. With operations spanning Kanpur, Ghaziabad, and several other cities, our reach grows every year — powered entirely by passionate people like you.

What We Do

Our Causes

From classrooms to clinics, from feeding drives to skill workshops — every effort counts.

📚

Education for All

We run learning drives, tutoring camps, and scholarship support to ensure every child gets access to quality education regardless of their background.

🏥

Healthcare Outreach

From free health check-up camps to blood donation drives and medicine distribution, we bring healthcare to doorsteps that clinics can't reach.

🍱

Food & Nutrition

No one should sleep hungry. Our food relief programs provide meals to underprivileged families, migrant workers, and elderly citizens across our operational cities.

👩‍💼

Women Empowerment

We run skill-building workshops, self-defence training, and awareness campaigns to help women stand confident and independent in society.

🌱

Environment & Cleanliness

Plantation drives, cleanliness campaigns, and awareness initiatives to build communities that are as clean and green as they are kind.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑

Community Building

Festivals, cultural programs, and neighbourhood events that bring people together and remind us that our strength lies in our unity.

If we all do something, then together there is no problem that we cannot solve!

How You Can Help

You don't need much — just the will to make a difference. Pick your way to contribute.

One-time Donation

Give what you can — any amount, however small, goes directly into programs that feed, educate, and heal.

Monthly Giving

Set up a monthly contribution and create a steady stream of support for our ongoing initiatives. Fully tax-exempted under 80G.

In-kind Donations

Books, clothes, stationery, medicines — material donations are just as powerful. Contact us to arrange a pickup or drop-off.

Teach & Tutor

📸

Creative Skills
